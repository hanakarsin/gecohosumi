A health emergency is not something that you want to experience with an empty pocket. That's why you need to have quick access to the top quality medical care in Nigeria. Health insurance companies in Nigeria

Health insurance covers the medical expenses of a patient. It spreads the risk over a large number of people involved in the health insurance company. The company estimates the overall risk of health system and health care expenses in Nigeria. According to these expenses, the health insurance company calculates and provides the payroll or tax to pay monthly by clients. The sum of monthly payroll is specified in an agreement between an insurance company and its clients.

In Nigeria, the National Health Insurance scheme was established in 2004. It was set up by the Health Maintenance Organizations in Nigeria. Private medical establishments now have a new and different way of providing medical care.

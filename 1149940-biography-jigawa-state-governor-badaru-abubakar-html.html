Biography of Jigawa state governor Badaru Abubakar

It is time to get more information about current Jigawa governor Badaru Abubakar. In this article we will review his way and all his merits. Some facts may really surprise you.

Dadaru Abubakar and his way to politics

Alh Mohammed Badaru Abubakar is current governor of Jigawa State since April 2015. This time we are going to talk about the biography of Jigawa governor.

Abubakar was born in 1962, his birth palace is Babura city. He studied at Bello University and graduated with a bachelor degree. In 1987 he gained the position of an Auditor in Audit Department of the Finance Ministry of Kano State.

In 1991 Abubakar Mohammed established his own business with Talamiz Company. The company after years of work was developed in Talamiz Nigeria Limited. The company has become successful and developed in many subsidiaries as a vast conglomerate.

After this business success, Badaru Abubakar became the Chairman of such companies as Socar Talamiz Limited, AML Bonded Terminal, Oil Mill. He is the holder of traditional titles such as Wali of Jahun in Jigawa State and Sardauna of Ringim Emirate.

Life after the election

Future Jigawa state government had experience of unsuccessful election in 2011. In 2015 the candidate of All Progressives Congress, Mohammed Abubakar has become a winner of governorship election.  

One of the first main tasks for a new governor was to solve the problem with water shortage in the state and to create an appropriate environment for investors. Many policy projects have already been crowned with success. In particular, much efforts were made to work with water supply in the region. At the second year on the position of governor he followed people-oriented programmes. Jigawa state governor has a family with three wives and they are blessed with children.

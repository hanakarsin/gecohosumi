15 Best science courses to study in Nigeria and their cut-off mark

Top 15 best science courses to study today

Here are the top 15 most popular courses under science and their cut-off mark.

1. Nursing

Nursing is one of the most popular courses in Nigeria that many students pursue. Nursing responsibilities can range from making acute treatment decisions to providing school inoculations. Several universities in Nigeria offer various nursing courses.

The cut-off mark for nursing programs can vary depending on the institution, but it is generally between 200 and 280.

2. Medicine and Surgery

Medicine and surgery is arguably Nigeria's most prestigious and competitive science course. As a medical professional, you can make a significant positive impact on people's lives.

The course in medicine and surgery is available in several Nigerian universities. Depending on the school's preference, it is typically housed within the Faculty of Health Sciences or Clinical Sciences.

The cut-off mark for admission into medicine programs in Nigerian universities can be as high as 350 in the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME).

3. Pharmacy

Pharmacy is another highly competitive science course. Those pursuing a pharmacy course can work in many dynamic fields, such as hospital pharmacy, industrial pharmacy, pharmaceutical sales, drug development and research. The minimum JAMB cut-off mark for a pharmacy to participate in the post-UTME is generally 200.

4. Dentistry

Dentistry is a challenging course yet rewarding. Dentistry plays a crucial role in maintaining and promoting oral health. Dental professionals diagnose and treat various oral conditions, including cavities, gum diseases, and infections.

Regular dental check-ups, cleanings, and preventive treatments help prevent oral health issues and improve overall well-being. The minimum cut-off mark for one to pursue a dentistry course is 200.

5. Computer Science

Computer science is a rapidly growing field with excellent job opportunities. Computer scientists research and analyze how computers store and use information.

Graduates find employment in diverse sectors such as computer software companies, game development, financial services, government, research, social media companies, and manufacturing. The cut-off mark for computer science programs can range from 180 to 250.

6. Electrical/Electronics Engineering

Electrical and electronics engineering is a broad field with applications in power generation, telecommunications, and many other industries. Electrical engineering deals with studying and applying electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. The cut-off mark for this course is typically 240 and above.

7. Biochemistry

Biochemistry is the study of the chemical processes that occur in living organisms. It is an interdisciplinary course of biology and chemistry, divided into three broad fields: molecular genetics, protein science, and metabolism. To pursue a biochemistry course in Nigeria, you must achieve a cut-off mark of 200 and above.

8. Chemical and Petroleum Engineering

Nigeria's oil wealth makes petroleum engineering a popular choice for science students. Numerous petroleum companies are paying well in Nigeria; hence, this course ranks among the most high-paying jobs. The cut-off mark for this course can be as high as 250 or more.

9. Microbiology

Microbiology is the study of the biology of microscopic organisms - viruses, bacteria, algae, fungi, slime moulds, and protozoa. This versatile science course has applications in various fields, such as healthcare, food production, and environmental science.

The general cut-off mark for microbiology programs is above 180. Microbiology is among the top ten 4 years science courses list in Nigeria students can pursue.

10. Geology

Geology studies the Earth's structure, composition, and history. The geology course is exciting, and if you love the science of the Earth and are curious about it, it will be a great course for you. The cut-off mark for geology programs can range from 160 to 240.

11. Physics

Physics is the fundamental science that seeks to understand the laws and principles governing everything in the universe, from the tiniest particles to the largest galaxies. By studying physics, you gain a deeper appreciation for the world around you and how it works.

A physics course will enable you to study classical mechanics, electromagnetism, thermodynamics, and quantum mechanics. Common cut-off marks for a physics program in Nigerian universities can range from 180 and above.

12. Agricultural Science

Agriculture is the science and practice of cultivating plants and livestock for food and other products. A degree in the field will open up various professional prospects, depending on your chosen educational path. The cut-off mark for agriculture programs can vary depending on the institution, but it is usually 160 and above.

13. Mathematics

Mathematics is the scientific study of quality, structure, space, and change. Mathematics is widely used in various domains, including natural science, engineering, medical, and social sciences. The cut-off mark for mathematics programs in Nigeria is typically between 180 and 230.

14. Veterinary Medicine

Veterinary medicine plays a crucial role in maintaining the health and well-being of various animals, including pets, livestock, and wildlife. Veterinarians are in high demand in Nigeria, and graduates can work in various research and production fields. The general cut-off mark for veterinary courses is around 180 for most Nigerian universities.

15. Political Science

Political science is one of the most popular social science courses marketable in Nigeria today. This course focuses on the theory and practice of government and politics at the local, state, national, and international levels.

Individuals who have completed a course in political science can pursue various career paths in both the public and private sectors. The cut-off marks for this course range from 190 and above.

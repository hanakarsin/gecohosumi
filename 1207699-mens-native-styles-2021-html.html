Native styles for men: Latest designs for 2024

Nigeria has talented fashion designers who have gained international recognition in recent years. Many of them have come up with unique dress styles for men, usually based on the traditional outfits for various communities. Read on to discover the native styles you should wear in 2024.

Latest white native styles for guys

Did you know that white symbolizes perfection and balance? These outfits are among the top latest styles for men because they portray a man that is composed and sure of himself. You will stand out if you wear a white outfit to a casual or formal function. Be cautious when wearing white to weddings as you do not want to outshine the bride on her special day.

Black and white looks

Are you looking for the best native style for guys for formal or casual events? You cannot go wrong with black and white outfits. They are fashionable and trendy. You can choose to have black as the primary color and break the monotony with white features or have it the other way around.

Amazing Agbada designs for 2024

Agbada refers to a flowing wide-sleeved robe and is men's native style in Yorubaland. The attire has been worn for centuries, but it is more stylish today. An experienced tailor can custom-make one in any colour and using any fabric. In most cases, this latest men's native style is done in bold colours with amazing decorations to make you stand out. However, you can choose a toned-down look and still wow everyone.

Bold native styles for males

Nigerian men are known for not shying away from bold and bright colours. The list of the latest styles for men can not be complete without outfits made from fabrics with bold patterns and prints. Stripes are particularly common in Nigeria.

Ankara-inspired outfits

The list of the latest native styles for guys in 2024 cannot be complete without traditional Ankara designs. Ankara is widely used in Nigeria, and the fabric comes in thousands of beautiful prints and colours. Men have a choice between full Ankara outfits or blending the Ankara with other fabrics.

Colourful senator styles for men

The latest senator styles for guys are simply classy, sophisticated, and smart. The design was popularised by Senator Anyim Pius Anyim, an Igbo chief and a national figure, who wore the attire when he was the senate president under President Olusegun Obasanjo's government. If you attend a formal function, you can choose a toned-down colour. If you want to stand out, pick one of the colourful options above.

How Designers Combine Tradition and Modern Native Styles

Aliyu also explained that designers blend traditional fabrics with modern native styles by combining classic elements with contemporary looks. He shared some of these approaches, including:

  • Fusion of Styles: Designers blend traditional fabrics with modern cuts and silhouettes, creating a fusion of traditional and contemporary styles. This allows for a harmonious balance between cultural authenticity and current fashion trends.
  • Innovative Tailoring: Traditional fabrics are often used innovatively through modern tailoring techniques. This could involve incorporating traditional patterns into modern garment designs or creating new patterns inspired by traditional motifs.
  • Mix and Match: Designers experiment with combining traditional fabrics with other materials to create a unique and eclectic look. This mix-and-match approach allows for creative expression while maintaining a connection to cultural roots.
  • Accessorizing: Integrating traditional fabrics through accessories like scarves, shawls, or embellishments on modern clothing is a subtle yet effective way to infuse cultural elements into contemporary attire.
  • Embroidery and Embellishments: Traditional embroidery techniques and embellishments are often applied to modern fabrics, adding a touch of cultural richness to contemporary designs.
  • Color Palette: Designers may draw inspiration from traditional color palettes when creating modern styles. This ensures a connection to cultural roots while keeping the overall design fresh and relevant.
  • Adaptation of Traditional Garments: Modernizing classic traditional garments by incorporating them into everyday wear helps bridge the gap between heritage and current fashion preferences.

How to check if driver's license is ready for collection

Here we are to tell you how to check driver's license status, and how you can collect the document. FRSC created a very convenient service, and anyone can check if his or her DL is ready just with the help of a phone. So, read on to see the method.

How to check driver's license status with the help of your phone

You can easily check the information about the collection of driver's license by following steps:

Type ‘NDL STATUS License number‘ and send it to the number 33811. This option is available for all networks in Nigeria. After sending such a message, you will receive all the information about your driver's license.

Note that it costs N50 for one text message. It is a very convenient, there is no need to visit the Federal Road Safety Commission office when you want to check the status of your driver's license.

When you receive confirmation that your DL is ready for collection, go to Driver’s License Center with your ID and be ready to enjoy driver’s life.

Is it possible to collect driver's license on behalf? Yes, it is possible. In this case, you need to write an authorization letter and send someone to pick up your license on your behalf.

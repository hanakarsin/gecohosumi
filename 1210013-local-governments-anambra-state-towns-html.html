Local governments in Anambra state and their towns

Local governments provide services to the population in the areas of education, culture, health care, social welfare, public parks, water supply, and sewage. All of them operate by decrees which are supplementing national laws and approved by the Ministry of Internal Affairs. This post will tell you about local governments in Anambra state and their towns. Below is all the information you need to know. Keep reading and find all the answers to your questions.

Local government in Anambra state

Do you know how many local government in Anambra state? Their total number is 21. Below we will tell you more about each of them.

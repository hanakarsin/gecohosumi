Comedian Basketmouth shares adorable photo of his kids

- Comedian Basketmouth has shared an adorable photo of his kids having fun in the United States

- The comedian shared the photo and joked about how his kids spent his money

- Basketmouth noted that his kids would be spending their next summer holiday in the village instead of outside Nigeria

Nigerian popular comedian Bright Okpocha, known by his stage name Basketmouth, has shared an adorable photo of his two kids while they were having fun on vacation in the United States.

The comedian took to his Instagram page to share the cute photo of his kids, Janelle and Jason Okpocha.

In the photo, the two kids were photo jumping and laughing excitedly. The comedian also joked about how his kids were jumping in the photo and the expense of the trip.

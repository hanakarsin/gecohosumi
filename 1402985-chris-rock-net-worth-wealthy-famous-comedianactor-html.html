Chris Rock net worth: how wealthy is the famous comedian/actor?

Chris Rock is an American comedian, actor, writer, producer, and director. He began his career as a stand-up comedian and later on progressed into acting and producing. So what is Chris Rock net worth?

Rock has been the face of comedy for many years and has appeared in films, mostly supporting roles.

As a comedian, he covers various genres, including black culture, human sexuality, racism and race relations, topical sociopolitical issues, and marriage.

Who is Chris Rock?

Chris Rock was born as Christopher Julius Rock in Andrews, South Carolina, on February 7, 1965, to Julius Rock and Rosalie. He was raised in Brooklyn, New York, and later moved to Bedford, Stuyvesant.

He schooled at James Madison High School in Brooklyn, New York. He, later on, dropped from school due to bullying.

Career as a comedian

Chris started doing standup comedy in 1984. He released his first comedy album in 1991.

Rock’s popularity grew, and he began featuring on TV shows, including as host of 1997, 1999, and 2003 MTV Video Music Awards. He followed up with three HBO comedy specials.

Between 1997 and 2000, he created a comedy talk show which ran for five seasons.

On October 30, 2016, Netflix announced the release of two new stand-up specials from Rock, for which he was reportedly paid $40 million ($20 million per special).

The first one, Chris Rock: Tamborine, was released on February 14, 2018, his first concert special in ten years.

After his breakthrough, he started starring in various shows, which made him the most acclaimed and successful comedian in the industry.

Chris got in big-screen success in the late 90's when he started receiving significant roles in films.

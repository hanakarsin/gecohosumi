South Africa's female athlete Caster Semenya and wife light up the internet with graduation photos

- Caster Semenya graduated from the North-West University with a Diploma in Sports Science

- The athlete warmed hearts on social media after sharing photos of herself with her wife during her graduation ceremony

- Her latest achievement came months after she made headlines with her sexual orientation

South Africa's female runner Caster Semenya has added another feather on her hat after finally graduating from a South African university.

Semenya, who burst to the limelight in 2009 when she won the female 800 meter race in Berlin, graduated from the North-West University in Potchefstroom with a Diploma in Sports Science.

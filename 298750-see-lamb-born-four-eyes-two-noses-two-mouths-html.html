A baby lamb with the unique deformity - two faces - was recently born in Slope Point, New Zealand.

The unusual lamb has immediately become one of the favourite animals of the farmer's, Justine Parker, children. The girls called it Two Face as it had 'two of everything': four eyes, two noses and two mouths.

Two Face's deformities have only caused him a few issues, he tilts slightly to the left when he walks and has some difficult feeding. Two Face appears to be a happy mutant lamb who interacts with the other farm animals like normal.

"We really care for him like one of our own now. He's just a bit of a dude. We do know - and the kids know - it's not going to live forever. But for it to live this long is a bit of a miracle really," Ms Parker said.

How did Linus Gabriel Sebastian start off?

After his earlier mentioned jobs, he first worked as a category manager at NCIX. He started working there in the year 2007. While still there, he used to post videos of himself reviewing different tech products.

In 2013, together with his two partners, they started Linus Media Group. From the company, they launched three channels:

  • Linus Tech Tips: This channel was started on the 24th November the year 2008.
  • Techquickie: This was the second channel he created. It was established in 2012. The main aim of this platform is to offer information about cool technology in just a few minutes.
  • Channel Super Fun: This channel was created for discussing new technology as well as having Q&A sessions with the viewers. It was created in May 2014.

Makueni man shoots thief with arrow only to discover it was his son

A suspected thief in Kalili village, Makueni county, has been left nursing injuries after he was shot with an arrow by his father.

Musyoka Kavoo, 40, was shot on his right hand by his father Kavoo Ngeta Musoli.

Kavoo said he heard someone break into his house, something that prompted him to get out and check while fully armed with a bow and an arrow.

He said he watched as the man was stealing windows which were stored in his house before he decided to shoot at him.

Kavoo said he raised alarm to alert the neighbours after shooting the man.

It is when the neighbours came that he decided to go check who the thief was and in disbelief, found out it was his son, Musyoka.

The suspect was rushed to Makindu hospital with the arrow dodged on his right hand, six inches deep.

Neighbours claimed Musyoka is a notorious thief who has been terrorising them.

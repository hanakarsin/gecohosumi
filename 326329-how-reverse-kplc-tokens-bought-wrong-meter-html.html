How to reverse KPLC tokens bought for the wrong meter

Prepaid meters are nowadays used by millions of Kenyans. This type of metering makes it possible for you to monitor your usage and spending. However, sometimes, when topping up units, you can enter the wrong account number resulting in an incorrect transaction. Is there a way in which you can reverse KPLC tokens bought for an erroneous meter?

Well, if you have purchased KPLC prepaid tokens to a wrong account number, you don’t need to panic. The unit code message will be sent to your phone, making you the legitimate owner of the units. How can you reverse KPLC tokens?

Procedure to reverse KPLC tokens

Use the steps below to reverse KPLC transaction at the comfort of your seat:

Method 1: Contacting KPLC via social media

Once you realize that you have entered a wrong account number, go to Facebook, or even Twitter to reach out to customer care.

You will have to share the transaction details to them and also the correct meter number you intended to buy tokens for. Kenya Power and Lighting representatives will then forward the details to the revenue office responsible for reversals.

Method 2: Visiting Kenya Power and Lighting offices

If you don’t have access to their social media platforms, you can choose to visit Kenya Power and Lighting customer care for KPLC token refund. Carry your national identity card, the correct meter number, and all the transaction details along with you. You will be required to fill a relocation form.

Method 3: Reversing Kenya Power and Lighting tokens via calling customer care

If you don’t know the location of Huduma Center or Kenya Power and Lighting offices near your place and you don’t have access to social media, you can opt to call their customer care directly from your phone for reversal. It is the fastest method to have your tokens refunded.

How long will KPLC tokens reversal take?

Once your details are forwarded to the revenue office, it will take approximately three working days to get a refund. For this reason, it is advisable to purchase some tokens as you wait for the reversal.

Once the reversal is done, you will receive the units' codes via SMS.

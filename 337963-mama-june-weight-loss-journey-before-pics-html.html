Mama June weight loss journey: Before and after pics

Mama June weight loss journey is one of the most dramatic weight loss transformations recorded in the history of mankind. The Honey Boo Boo's mum took extreme measures to achieve the ideal body size. Her goal for this transformational journey was to fit into a long-sleeved, size 4 dress, which she planned to wear during her ex's wedding. She has achieved this goal, and her appearance has been transformed for the better.

Mama June, whose real name is June Shannon, embarked on the weight loss journey in the year 2016. At the time, she used to weigh 460 pounds. She managed to lose 300 pounds within a period of one year. Mama June transformation happened in the public limelight. WEtv documented her in the series "Mama June: From Not to Hot".

Mama June weight loss journey

In 2016, she weighed 460 pounds. She used to wear a size 18 dress. Honey Boo Boo's mum had ballooned up following years of binge-eating junk food and not exercising. The wake-up call to lose weight came when she was handed a wedding invitation by her ex, Mike Thompson.

Mama June weight loss surgery

In 2016, Mama June made the decision to undergo surgery. This was after she fainted after regular dieting, which put her at risk of health problems. She had already lost 80 pounds, but she was not satisfied with the progress she had made. The biggest catalyst for change in her weight loss journey was the gastric sleeve surgery that was performed on her stomach in May 2016. During this procedure, her stomach size was reduced by about 75 percent. This helped her to feel fuller for longer and forced her to reduce caloric intake.

Mama June after surgery

After losing a lot of weight through surgery, her stomach started looking gross from the fast weight loss results. Like many other gastric sleeve surgery patients, she opted to undergo surgery to remove the skin on her stomach.

Did Mama June use a special diet to lose weight?

Mama June had to make a lot of swaps in her diet to shed off the extra pounds. At the time when she made the decision to embark on the weight loss journey in 2016, she had already cut out soda from her diet. Her favorite drink was Go-Go juice. She was forced to switch to healthy alternatives such as water and a liquid diet that consisted of green smoothies. Mama June admitted that she did not like to eat green vegetables, so taking them in liquid form was a plus for her.

How much weight has Mama June gained back?

In August 2018, she surprised her fans when she revealed she had gained back 50 pounds. This brought her weight to 200 pounds. Even though she faces a great challenge in maintaining this body size, she is determined to never again weigh 460 pounds. Responding to her critics who think that she is going to gain the weight back, she said:

I've gained a little bit of weight back but I’m not ever going to be 450 pounds again.

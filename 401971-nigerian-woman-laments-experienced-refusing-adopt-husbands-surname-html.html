Woman laments over what she experienced after refusing to adopt husband's surname

- A woman identified as Aisha Salaudeen said she was harshly judged by immigration staff because she refused to adopt her husband's surname

- According to Aisha, she was at the immigration office to renew her passport but the staff could not stop talking about the decision she made

- The lady said she decided to keep the name because she has had it all her life, adding that all her achievements are contained in it

A Nigerian woman identified as Aisha Salaudeen recently took to social media to express displeasure over what she experienced at the immigration office because of her refusal to adopt her husband's surname.

She said she was judged harshly by immigration staff who could not stop talking about her decision to keep her surname.

Aisha tweeted:

"I was judged harshly by immigration staff when I went to renew my international passport for keeping my name. Refusing to bear my husband's name was so bizarre to them that they refused to shut up about it."

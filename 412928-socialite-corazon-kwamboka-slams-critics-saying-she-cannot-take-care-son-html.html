Socialite Corazon Kwamboka Slams Critics Saying She Cannot Take Care of Son

- Kwamboka said she was tired of people always advising her on how to raise her son Taiyari

- She disclosed that people usually go to her with advice whenever she posts photos and videos of her son

- She threatened to block anyone who will ever advise her on how to take care of Taiyari

Kenyan socialite Corazon Kwamboka has clapped back at people who have been ''teaching'' her how to raise her son Taiyari.

Kwamboka expressed her displeasure at people always criticising her on how she has been taking care of her son.

The mother of one through a recent video on her Instagram stories said some people have always been giving her all manner of advice whenever she posts photos and videos of Taiyari.

Kwamboka who is also a lawyer told off people and warned them about giving her advice on how to raise her baby.

She used the opportunity to disclose that her baby has been dealing with a breathing condition called adenoids which is quite a common one among toddlers.

The mum noted the condition has been giving her son difficulties in breathing but has a paediatrician who is treating him.

”Something I have noticed, every time I post my baby, some bloody fools, idiots, lunatics, will come to my page and start telling me; 'Oh, mbona uyu mtoto anakuanga na homa kila siku…This baby is having difficulty breathing. Sijui do this, do that. You guys are idiots… You are a fool if you think that me Corazon is on my page posting my baby waiting for your opinion because I don’t see a problem with what he has…Do not come to my page trying to give me advice on what to do… People should mind their business” Kwamboka said.

The curvy madam wrapped up by warning her ''advisors'' to keep off or get blocked.

Just recently, TUKO.co.ke reported how Kwamboka refuted rumours that she had broken up with her baby daddy Frankie Kiarie aka FrankieJustGymIt.

Rumours had been going round that the bodacious mother of one and the fitness trainer's relationship was on the rocks after she shared a message, talking about starting over.

"Start the f**k over if you have to," she posted.

In an exclusive interview with TUKO.co.ke, she denied the rumours, saying:

"We (Frankie and I) are even together now. Those are just rumours. The post (first one) is about GENiO, my page was disabled and had to start a new page."

Kwamboka also rubbished rumours they unfollowed each other on Instagram a few days ago.

Mom Loses Two Sons to Coronavirus 12 Hours Apart, Urges Parents to Ensure Kids Are Vaccinated

  • A US woman is regretting not having forced her two sons to take the COVID-19 vaccine
  • The woman, Lisa Brandon, of Jacksonville, said that her two sons succumbed to the virus
  • She said that her sons had contracted the virus in July and had been in hospital since

After her two sons aged 35 and 41 died of COVID-19 related complications, Lisa Brandon is a mom in pain.

The two sons, Aaron, 35, and Free 41, were diagnosed with COVID-19 in July, and their conditions quickly became worse earlier this month.

Call for vaccination

The two then died within 12 hours of each other, leaving the family completely shattered.

The heartbroken mom is now calling upon parents to ensure their kids get vaccinated.

Speaking to The Mirror, the mom said that she desperately regrets not pushing her sons to get vaccinated when she had a chance.

Mom Felt totally helpless

She said that she felt totally helpless as her children died after contracting the deadly virus.

Both her sons died within half a day of each other, leaving their families totally devastated.

“I think they would be alive today if they would have got their shot. I wish I could have made them do it. And now it’s too late. It’s a parent’s worst nightmare. The only reason I’m doing this appeal is to put the word out to please get vaccinated," she said.

According to a report by the Mirror, both sons were on ventilators and Free was placed in a medically induced coma at the beginning of August.

Family loses three to COVID-19

Earlier this month, a family in Murang'a lost three of its members within a week, TUKO.co.ke reported.

According to a report by TV 47, the family members were suspected of having been infected with COVID-19 by a villager who contracted the disease from a burial he attended in Western Kenya.

The patient reportedly infected more than 10 members of one family. As a result, James Mwangi lost his father, grandfather and step-father.

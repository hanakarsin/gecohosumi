Mzungu Woman Praises Black Man for Treating Her Daughter Well: &quot;He Takers Her to Church&quot;

  • A mzungu woman has reacted to her daughter dating a black boy after her daughter changed her profile picture on social media
  • The woman describing the black boy said he interacts with courtesy and always attaches the title madam to each statement he says to her
  • She gushed about the way the black boy treats her daughter, something that has never happened to her

A mzungu woman named Heather Boyer has stated that she is comfortable with her daughter being in a relationship with a black boy.

Heather's reaction came after someone sent her a text that reads "I didn’t know she was dating a black boy, did you?” and she noticed the young lady changed her profile picture on social media.

Lots of love

According to Heather, the boy shows her daughter so much love. She said he takes the daughter on dates, ballgames, partying on weekends and even to church.

Nollywood Single Mothers Who Have Found Love Again

Once bitten twice shy is the popular saying but these actresses have been able to give marriage another chance despite the fact that they have been heartbroken before. Legit.ng brings you celebrated screen divas who have remarried after their heart were broken.

Ibinabo Fiberesima

She is a mother of five lovely children, four boys and a girl. She had them for three different fathers, the last child she had for fellow Nollywood actor Fred Amata but all are with her. The actress cum beauty queen and franchise owner of Miss Earth found love again in Uche Egbuka and they got married in December, 2014.

Rose Oransanye-Odika

Rose had a daughter from a marriage that did not work. Her lovely daughter stays with her and in her absence moves in with her mother. Both Rose and her mum stay in Ibadan. Rose in October announced that she will be getting married to another man before the end of this year.

Foluke Daramola

Foluke Daramola got married to her erstwhile husband, Tunde Sobowale, in 2005 but their marriage ended in 2008. Their marriage produced two children, a boy and a girl who are in the custody of Foluke. Daramola found love again in the arms of Kayode Salako whom she later married on February 15, 2013.

Stella Damasus

Her case was not intentional nor was it man made, but for the death of her loving husband, Jaiye Aboderin fondly referred to as Jaye Jeje, be it as it may she made the list. Damasus had two lovely daughters for Jaiye Jeje before his death. Even though she contracted another marriage with Lagos socialite, Lanre Nzeribe but the marriage hit the rock in no time like a badly arranged pack of cards. Stella later found love with Daniel Ademinokan, a movie director who was once married to fellow actress Doris Simeon with whom he had a son, David.

Ronke Ojo

Ronke Ojo is a mother of two children. Ronke Oshodi-Oke as she is fondly referred to by her fans also belongs to this class of actresses because she had her first child, a daughter to another man before she got married to Anthony Gbolahan in January 2014.they had a son together.

Mercy Aigbe

The former banker turned actress has been married and had a daughter before the marriage crashed. She later found love in the arm of Lanre Gentry the Ikoyi registry, Lagos in 2013. They have a son together.

Emem Isong

The Nollywood Film maker, writer and CEO of Royal Arts Academy, Emem Isong was a single mother of one before she met her husband. The Akwa Ibom prolific film director has a son from a crashed relationship. She later met and married her Cameroonian heartthrob, Akama Misodi on December 6, 2014.

Bukky Wright

Beautiful screen diva, Bukky Wright is a mother of two big boys, Eniola and Gbenga. The boys she had for her first husband, Gboyega Amu. Even though she had a second marriage to a media practitioner, the marriage did not produce any issue. Bukky later got married to Bolaji Saheed aka Bolaji Basia through an Islamic Nikkai in 2005. Even though she has two boys and after a third attempt at marriage Bukky who is still looking smashing married Adewale Onitiri at Trenton registry, New Jersey Registry in 2011.

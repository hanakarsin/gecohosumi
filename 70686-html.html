Pastor Ashimolowo's Son Spends Honeymoon With Wife In Dubai

Son of Senior Pastor of Kingsway International Christian Centre (KICC) and his wife, Dorothy Jeneba Kamara, shared some photos from their honeymoon.

Tomi Ashimolowo and Sierra Leone beauty Dorothy Jeneba Kamara got married on Saturday, July 26, in Kent, UK. The couple are now on their honeymoon in Dubai.

Dorothy Jeneba Kamara took to her Instagram page to share some sweet photos from the honeymoon. The newlywed hottie showed off her body in a swimwear during a visit to the beach with her husband.

It would be recalled that the father of the groom, Matthew Ashimolowo, who is a prominent Man Of God in Nigeria, was recently charged to a Lagos High Court, Igbosere, by four chieftaincy clans of Offiran and Aiyeteju villages in Lagos.

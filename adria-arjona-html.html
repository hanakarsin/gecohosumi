Adria Arjona - Age, Family, Bio

Puerto Rican actress most famous for her roles as Dorothy Gale on Emerald City and Emily on the second season of True Detective. She has also appeared in films like 2015's Wedding in New York and 2016's The Belko Experiment. She gained fame in 2022 when she played Bix Caleen in the Star Wars series Andor. 

Before Fame

She was raised in Mexico City until age 12, when she moved to Miami, Florida. She received acting training at The Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.

Trivia

She initially turned down the opportunity to audition for Emerald City because she did not believe she would be picked for the part. She has amassed over 25 acting credits throughout her developing career. 

Family Life

She is the daughter of singer-songwriter Ricardo Arjona.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Colin Farrell on True Detective.

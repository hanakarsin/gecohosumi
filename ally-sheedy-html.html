Ally Sheedy - Age, Family, Bio

Brat pack actress who appeared in the 1985 film The Breakfast Club. She received additional fame for her roles in High Art, War Games and St. Elmo's Fire. In 2022, she returned to acting in the Freeform show Single Drunk Female.

Before Fame

She wrote a children's book titled She Was Nice to Mice when she was 12.

Trivia

She played Sean Penn's girlfriend in Bad Boys. 

Family Life

She married David Lansbury in April of 1992. The couple welcomed a child named Rebecca in 1994 and eventually divorced in 2009.

Associated With

She was in The Breakfast Club with Emilio Estevez.

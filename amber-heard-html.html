Amber Heard - Age, Family, Bio

Actress and model whose claim to fame came in 2008 with lead roles in Pineapple Express and Never Back Down. She received additional fame after starring in The Stepfather, The River Why, The Ward, Drive Angry, The Rum Diary and Magic Mike XXL. In 2018, she was cast as Mera in Aquaman.

Before Fame

She was very involved in her high school's drama department before dropping out at age 17 to pursue modeling. 

Trivia

She starred in the short-lived TV series The Playboy Club. 

Family Life

She came out as bisexual at the 25th anniversary GLAAD event. She was married to actor Johnny Depp from 2015 until they filed for divorced just over a year later. In 2017, she began dating Elon Musk, but the two separated later that same year. In late 2019, she began dating Bianca Butti. Her daughter Oonagh Paige was born on April 8, 2021.

Associated With

Nicolas Cage costarred with her in Drive Angry.

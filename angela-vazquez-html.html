ngela Vzquez - Age, Family, Bio

Lead vocalist of the sibling trio band Vázquez Sounds alongside her brothers Abelardo and Gustavo. She is a multi-instrumentalist who plays piano, harmonica, and guitar in the band.

Before Fame

She and her brothers first blew up on the internet following the release of their cover of Adele's "Rolling in the Deep."

Trivia

She and her siblings released their self-titled debut album in 2012, with the album peaking at #4 in Mexico.

Family Life

She is from Mexicali, Baja California, Mexico. She made an Instagram post featuring her mother for Mother's Day 2020.

Associated With

She performed a cover of Mariah Carey's song "All I Want for Christmas Is You."

Arsene Wenger Predicts Winner of 2023-2024 Premier League Title

Former Arsenal manager, Arsene Wenger believes that the north London club will win the 2023-2024 Premier League title.

For the first time since Arsene Wenger left the Emirates Stadium, Arsenal were very close to winning the 2022-2023 Premier League title.

Coach Mikel Arteta and his boys occupied the first spot on the Premier League table for over 200 days in the 2022-2023 season before Manchester City overtook them in the last two months of the season.

At the end of the thrilling season, Manchester City won the Premier League over Arsenal with just one point and it is believed that it was the Gunners’ complacency that cost the club the title in the last minutes of the campaign.

Ahead of the 2023-2024 season, Arsenal have shown that they are ready for a better campaign by buying more prolific and experienced players.

Coach Mikel Arteta who seems determined to win the Premier League title has signed Kai Havertz, Jurrien Timber, and Declan Rice, a club record signing from West Ham United.

Based on Arsenal’s performance last season and the number of superstar players the North London side have signed so far this summer, Arsene Wenger believes the coming Premier League season is for the Gunners.

“I believe we will win the championship; it is as simple as that. I have fewer doubts for Arsenal than the cycling today”, Arsene Wenger said during the kick-off of Stage 15 of the Tour de France.

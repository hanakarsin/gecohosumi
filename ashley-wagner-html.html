Ashley Wagner (Figure Skater) - Age, Family, Bio

American figure skater who won a bronze medal at the 2014 Winter Olympics. She also won a silver medal at the 2016 World Championships.

Before Fame

She lived in six states and two countries growing up on military bases as a child.

Trivia

She was also the 2012 Skate America, Four Continents, and Trophee Eric Bompard champion and won consecutive U.S. National Championships in 2012 and 2013.

Family Life

She has a younger brother named Austin, who is also a competitive skater.

Associated With

She defeated teen Hannah Miller to win gold at the 2013 U.S. National Championships.

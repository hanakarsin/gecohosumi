Briana DeJesus - Age, Family, Bio

Best recognized for her appearances alongside her daughter Nova Star on MTV's hit series Teen Mom 2.

Before Fame

She first made her reality debut during the fourth season of MTV's 16 and Pregnant.

Trivia

Her sister, Brittany, also became pregnant around the time that Briana did during 16 and Pregnant, but her sister chose to get an abortion.

Family Life

Her sister, Brittany DeJesus, has become an Instagram sensation. Her daughter's father is Devoin Austin II. She welcomed a second daughter, Stella, in July 2017. She got engaged to her fiancé, tattoo artist Javi Gonzalez, in May 2021.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Mackenzie McKee, Alex Sekella, and Katie Yeager on Teen Mom 3.

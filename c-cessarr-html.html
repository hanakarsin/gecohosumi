Cesar Martinez - Age, Family, Bio

Interactive comedy content creator who is recognized for posting short videos on his c.cessarr TikTok account. His point-of-view style videos have garnered him a fanbase of more than 1.4 million followers. He has posted multiple POV videos where he acts like he is playing the spin the bottle game.  

Before Fame

His oldest archived TikTok featured the "I'm Not a Model, But" trope.

Trivia

He ridiculed a TikTok algorithm change in an early 2020 video. He posts selfies and other moody photos of himself to his c.cessarr Instagram account, which has earned over 70,000 followers. He could often be seen wearing tank tops or posing shirtless.

Family Life

He has two brothers named Alberto and Ari. 

Associated With

He created a duet video featuring Corey Whelan.

'Could She Be Their Daughter?'

Nigerians on social media have reacted to the video of music producer, Don Jazzy’s ex-wife Michelle Jackson, an American model with her teen daughter as they reunite with her ex-husband.

Naija News earlier reported that the music producer took to his Instagram story to share video from a dinner date he hosted his ex-wife and her teen daughter to in Lagos.

According to Don Jazzy, they have been spending a lot of time together since her return.

However, curious fans could not help but link the striking resemblance between Michelle Jackson’s teen daughter and the music producer.

Some fans opined that the teenage girl looked so much like Don Jazzy.

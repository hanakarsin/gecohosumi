Dayna Marie - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok star who is best recognized for her prank videos and lifestyle content. Often in malls, with groups of her friends, or surprising people on the street she shares her mainly prank driven content for her over 6 million fans. Her "the truth about our breakup...." video about her relationship received over 500 thousand views since it was posted on YouTube in June of 2020. 

Before Fame

She first started sharing comedy driven content to TikTok in March of 2019. 

Trivia

She is also an influencer and ambassador for several brands including Bracelets Dude, Nectar, Shop Wavey, and Prive Revaux. She starred in the music video for Bryce Vine's "Stay." She has modeled for MCM. 

Family Life

She has a sister named Heather who is a model. Her parents' names are Steve and Lisa. Steve is also popular on TikTok with over 100,000 followers. She was born in San Diego, and later moved to Hawaii with social media star Kouvr Annon, and then to Los Angeles. She and Jack Bono have been dating.

Associated With

She is friends with Emmy Combs and Jack Bono. She has appeared on David Dobrik's YouTube channel.

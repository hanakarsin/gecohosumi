Feb 25, 2024 Chauncey Koziol

Darkoo - Age, Family, Bio

Hip-hop artist who had a breakout 2019 when her single "Gangsta" went viral. Other songs including "Rover" and "Weh You Did Deh?" have helped her YouTube channel reach more than 150,000 subscribers.

Before Fame

She dropped the single "Friendzone" on SoundCloud in 2017.

Trivia

Her fans have supported her on Instagram where she shares lifestyle and backstage photos with 220,000 followers. In March 2022, she posted a TikTok of her dancing to her track "Always," which earned over 30 million views in less than one month. In 2020 she was nominated for a MOBO award for 'Best Female Act.'

Family Life

She was born in Nigeria and raised in South London, United Kingdom. She's talked about being gender-fluid.

Associated With

She has collaborated musically with rapper One Acen.

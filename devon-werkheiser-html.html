Devon Werkheiser - Age, Family, Bio

Best known as Ned Bigby on the television show Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide and his role in the TV movie Shredderman Rules.

Before Fame

He was given a supporting role in We Were Soldiers after being part of the Acting Factory.

Trivia

He released the hit song "If Eyes Could Speak" in 2010.

Family Life

His older sister is Vanessa Werkheiser. He dated actress Sara Montez.

Associated With

Lindsey Shaw co-starred with him in Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide.

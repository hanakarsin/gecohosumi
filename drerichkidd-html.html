Rxchie - Age, Family, Bio

Born Micah Javon Cooper, this popular rapper rose to fame during his childhood years, eventually founding the RichGang Music Group. His debut single, "Love More," was released in 2014 and was promoted by Radio Disney. 

Before Fame

He was involved in his church choir and played and acted in several local talent shows. He turned down a football scholarship to Jackson State in order to pursue music and acting.

Trivia

By 2014, he was worth well over a million dollars. He made his debut acting role in The Purge: Anarchy in 2014.

Family Life

A native of Los Angeles, California, he spent part of his childhood in Belzoni, Mississippi. His hip hop career was managed by his mother, Louise Cooper.

Associated With

He was greatly influenced by the work of fellow rapper Drake.

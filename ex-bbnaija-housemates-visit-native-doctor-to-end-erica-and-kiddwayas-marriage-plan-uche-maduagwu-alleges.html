Ex-BBNaija Housemates Visit Native Doctor To End Erica And Kiddwayas Marriage Plan

Nigerian movie actor, Uche Maduagwu has alleged that some former housemates of the Big Brother Naija reality show are visiting native doctors to ensure that billionaire son, Kiddwaya and his girlfriend Erica do not marry in the coming year.

Naija News understands that Kiddwaya and Erica had been spotted on different locations recently having their nice time together, a development which has been welcomed by their fans and parents partly.

However, Maduagwu in a post on his Instagram account on Monday, November 30, noted that some of the former BBNaija housemates are visiting ‘Babalawo’ houses to make sure the two lover birds never marry in Dubai in 2021.

He further prayed for his ‘Nigeria and Ghana fans never to be turned into lord of engagement ring’. Naija News understands that by this, he was referring to singer, Davido and Chioma’s relationship.

See Maduagwu’s assertion below;

“Some ex #bbnaija5 housemates are visiting babalawo to make sure #erica and #kiddwaya dont #marry next year in #dubai but i wont mention names on #instagram yet those who saw these ex #bbnaija2020 housemate mocking stargirl said it was a very shocking moment seeing it. What #davido could not do for chioma in many years even after a baby kiddwaya will do for erica next year because true #love is bigger than hype. I pray for all the #beautiful women reading this in #naija and #ghana may you never be turned into lord of engagement ring. #endsars”

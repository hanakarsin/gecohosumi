Fans Advise Groovy After Private Clip With Phyna Surfaces

A fan of a former housemate of the Big Brother Naija ‘Leve Up’ edition, Groovy has advised that he enjoys all the special treatments that come along with dating his love interest, Phyna, who’s the winner of the Level Up edition.

This is coming after Phyna who has been in a relationship with her colleague, Groovy went live on Instagram to share special moments she was having with her man on social media.

In the video sighted, a shy Groovy was captured trying to interact with fans of Phyna by making funny faces, in a way of connecting to them.

Reacting to the video, a fan identified as jesroland via the comment section of an Instagram page gave an unsolicited advise, he wrote: ”Groovy abeg help her finish the 50 m joor”

Another fan, Wendychyrystal wrote: ‘’if i was phyna and groovy i could be doing live everyday…infact they should see other reality shows where couples build brand together.’’

Although some netizens alleged differently about the relationship, saying Groovy was in it for the money, others felt the couple would become the first housemates who’d build a brand together.

Meanwhile, Phyna, has noted that her ex-boyfriend demanded he want her back after her life changed before going for the BBNaija show.

In her words: “I met this other one, we dated and we broke up. I relocated to Abuja, my lifestyle changed, I was doing things. From the Abuja now, I am in Big Brother’s house, now the guy has started, he even called me this morning that he wants me back.”

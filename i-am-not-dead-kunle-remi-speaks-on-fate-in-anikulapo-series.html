I Am Not Dead - Kunle Remi Speaks On Fate In Anikulapo Series

Nollywood actor, Kunle Remi has taken to his social media page to reveal his fate in Kunle Afolayan‘s 2022 award-winning movie, Anikulapo.

This comes after filmmaker Kunle Afolayan announced on Monday, 17th July, the end of the shooting of Anikulapo The Series.

The 48-year-old  had shared a video on his Instagram page that captured the moment the cast and crew of Anikulapo The Series screamed in excitement as he announced the end of shooting.

In the movie, Kunle, who played the lead role and was thought to have died, however, returns in the series Anikulapo: Rise of the Spectre.

Sharing a picture of himself on Instagram on Monday night, Kunle wrote, “As you can see, I am not dead.”

Kunle controversially missed out on nominations at the 2023 Africa Magic Viewers’ Choice Awards (AMVCA) where Anikulapo won five awards, including best overall movie.

The movie, which was directed and co-produced by Kunle Afolayan, starred actors such as Bimbo Ademoye, Kunle Remi, Sola Sobowale, Hakeem Kae-Kazim, Taiwo Hassan, Yinka Quadri, and Eyiyemi Afolayan, among others.

'If Tinubu Wins, Its Going To Be A Plus For The Youths And Nigeria'

Nigerian media personality Daddy Freeze has advised Nigerians to support their preferred political candidate without attacking one another.

This comes after Nigerian comedian Mr Jollof declared support for Bola Tinubu and was dragged online.

Reacting to a video of Mr Jollof saying he would still campaign for Tinubu despite the criticism, Daddy Freeze in a post on Instagram said Nigerian Youths need to work on freedom of political choice.

Daddy Freeze said he loves Peter Obi but this does not mean he doesn’t value Bola Tinubu, his political majesty and his vision as a leader.

The media personality added that he would support Peter Obi with whatever he can but if Tinubu wins, it’s still going to be a plus for the Youths and Nigeria.

He wrote: “My message to @mr.jollof_ One of the things Nigerian youths need to work on is freedom of political choice. I love Peteru and I am Obidient. This doesn’t mean I don’t value Ashiwaju, his political majesty and his vision as a leader.”

“I would vote for Peteru and support him with whatever I can, but if BAT wins, it’s still going to be a plus for the youths and for Nigeria.”

“Let’s make this election fun and May the best man win… let’s vote our respective candidates in love and peace.”

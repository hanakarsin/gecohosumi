IPOB Leader, Nnamdi Kanu Hires New Lawyer

The leader of the Indigenous People of Biafra, Nnamdi Kanu, has hired a new lawyer, Chukwuma Machukwu Umeh to strengthen his legal team.

Kanu is currently facing charges on terrorism, treasonable felony amongst other crimes filed against him by the Federal Government of Nigeria.

The development was disclosed in a post shared on Twitter by one of Kanu’s lawyers, Aloy Ejimakor, on Monday evening.

The lawyer to the IPOB leader disclosed that he just exited from a groundbreaking visitation with Kanu, adding that they covered many new grounds.

Ejimakor further stated that Kanu sends his love and felicitation to all his true followers.

He wrote, “just exited from a groundbreaking visitation with Onyendu Mazi Nnamdi Kanu.

“It was groundbreaking because we covered many new grounds, including the addition of Chukwuma Machukwu Umeh (SAN) to the Legal Team.

“Onyendu sends his love & felicitation to all his true followers.”

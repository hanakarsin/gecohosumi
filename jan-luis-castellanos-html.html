Jan Luis Castellanos - Age, Family, Bio

Television actor and model best recognized for his role as Diego in the Netflix show 13 Reasons Why. He is also known for his appearances on the shows Runaways and Don't Look Deeper. He has amassed over 10 acting credits throughout his career. 

Before Fame

While he was in high school he won the state wrestling championship title. 

Trivia

He has modeled for both Adidas and Abercrombie & Fitch. In 2021, he produced a short film titled "Influencia." 

Family Life

He was born in the Dominican Republic and is the eldest of four children. 

Associated With

He and Gregg Sulkin both appeared on the television show Runaways.

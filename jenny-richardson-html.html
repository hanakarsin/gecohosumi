Jenny Richardson - Age, Family, Bio

English actress best known for her role as Ethel Hallow in the series The Worst Witch. It was her debut acting role.

Before Fame

She did competitive gymnastics. She took home first place at a competition with her regional team in March of 2017.

Trivia

She was part of the Musical Theatre Squad at Laine theatre Arts for five months between 2017 and 2018.

Family Life

Her mom helped run her Instagram account, _jennyrichardson.

Associated With

She starred alongside Dagny Rollins in The Worst Witch.

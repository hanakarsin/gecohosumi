Jeremy Swift - Age, Family, Bio

English actor and musician known for appearing in the TV series Ted Lasso and Downton Abbey. He also starred in the 2018 film Mary Poppins Returns.

Before Fame

His first acting credit was in the 1983 film Fords on Water.

Trivia

He is also a composer who worked on the score for the 2009 TV movie Werewolves: The Dark Survivors.

Family Life

He married actress Mary Roscoe. They have two children.

Associated With

He played Spratt, the butler of the Dowager Countess played by Maggie Smith in Downton Abbey.

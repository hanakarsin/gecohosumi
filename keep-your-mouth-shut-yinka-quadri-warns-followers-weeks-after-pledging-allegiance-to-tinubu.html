Keep Your Mouth Shut - Yinka Quadri Warns Followers Weeks After Pledging Allegiance To Tinubu

Nollywood actor Yinka Quadri has warned his fans against making comments on issues they have little knowledge about.

The veteran actor took to Instagram to state that contributing to a discussion without having prior knowledge of the matter might be of grave consequences.

He wrote: “If you do not have complete knowledge about anything, better keep your mouth shut. You might think that speaking about something without full knowledge is a trivial matter. But it might have grave consequences. Morals.”

This is coming weeks after the actor, alongside his colleagues, namely Jide Kosoko, Yinka Quadri, Adebayo Salami, Femi Adebayo, Taiwo Hassan, Fausat Balogun, Fathia William, Eniola Ajao, Saheed Balogun, Murphy Afolabi, among others declared support for the presidential bid of the All Progressives Congress flag bearer, Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

The video shared on Quadri’s official Instagram handle, “Olayinkaquadrifilms” was accompanied by the following caption: “ASIWAJU BOLA AHMED TINUBU FOR PRESIDENT 2023.”

However, the video had been greeted with a lot of negative comments, as Nigerians berated the veteran and his colleagues for choosing to support Tinubu.

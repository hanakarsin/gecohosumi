Lydia Bright - Age, Family, Bio

British reality television personality on The Only Way Is Essex who also owns a clothing and accessory shop called Bella Sorella alongside her sister Georgia.

Before Fame

She attended a language school called Anglo European for secondary school due to her mom's Italian heritage. She was a member of the school orchestra there as a violinist.

Trivia

After spending time in several dead-end jobs, she moved to Marbella, Spain to have some fun working in a bar.

Family Life

She has siblings named Romana Bright, Georgia Bright and Freddie James Bright as well as three foster siblings. Her mother Debbie Bright worked in fashion.

Associated With

She began a relationship with fellow The Only Way is Essex star James Argent.

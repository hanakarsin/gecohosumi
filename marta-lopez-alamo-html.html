Marta Lpez lamo - Age, Family, Bio

Model who won the title of Miss World Jaen 2019, earning the right to compete in the Miss World Spain pageant. She has a strong social following, frequently sharing images from her modeling career with her 400,000 Instagram followers. 

Before Fame

She was discovered in 2012, when she entered into a modeling search in Barcelona. 

Trivia

She has been an ambassador for brands like Shein and Prozis España.

Family Life

She has a sister named Rosa who is a bikini fitness professional competitor. She has dated television personality and actor Kiko Matamoros. 

Associated With

She and Marina Ruiz have both represented Jaen at Miss World Spain.

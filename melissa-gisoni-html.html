Melissa Gisoni - Age, Family, Bio

Star of the series Dance Moms who is known for being the mother of Maddie and Mackenzie Ziegler. She appeared in the video for "Freaks Like Me" by Todrick Hall.

Before Fame

She made an impression right away on season one of Dance Moms when she claimed that her daughter Maddie was the best dancer. 

Trivia

While she regrets not going to college, she's expressed a desire for her daughters to perform on Broadway and go to college.

Family Life

She married Greg Gisoni in 2013. She is step-mom to his two kids, Mathew and Michele. She was previously married to Kurt Ziegler.

Associated With

She became good friends with Abby Lee Miller, owner of the Abby Lee Dance Company.

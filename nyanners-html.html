Nyanners - Age, Family, Bio

Voice actor and YouTuber most well known for posting dubs of popular manga to her self-titled YouTube channel. She has gained more than 1.4 million subscribers performing dubs, song covers, and playing games on her channel. She has grown popular on her Twitch channel where she streams many family friendly games.

Before Fame

She debuted her YouTube channel in April 2011 with the inaugural video "STAPLER SAN."

Trivia

Some of her most popular YouTube videos, like "NYANACONDA" and "I Am Ur Leader," have earned well over 1 million views a piece.

Family Life

Her real name is Taylor and she has often posted photos of her boyfriend Chad on her Instagram. 

Associated With

She has covered songs by a number of popular artists like Sia and Nicki Minaj.

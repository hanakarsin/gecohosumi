Pastor Enenches Daughter Breaks Silence On Her Alleged Relationship With Frank Edwards

The daughter of Pastor Paul Enenche of Dunamis International Gospel Center, Deborah Paul Enenche on Sunday broke her silence following the viral reports that she was engaged to gospel music star, Frank Edwards of Christ Embassy.

Naija News understands that there have been speculations that the Rocktown Records Label Chief Executive Officer (CEO) engaged the daughter of the Senior Pastor of the Dunamis International Gospel Center (DIGC), Dr Paul Enenche, after the young lady took to social media to share pictures of her engagement ring.

However, reacting to the speculations, Frank Edwards took to his Instagram handle to debunk the report.

“It is false news, let the girl enjoy her peace,” he wrote.

Hours later, the social media platform (Not that of Naija News) took down the post, tendering an apology for misinforming the public.

Speaking about the development, Deborah, who described the report as “best in fake news” stated that her fans “will hear everything soon”regarding her supposed relationship.

She added that, she had learned that the internet or social media is not where people should bring certain personal issues.

Deborah who also sings, wrote,” If you truly don’t want it to be discussed, don’t bring it up.

“Anything that can be misunderstood will be misunderstood.

“Don’t let anyone rush you. Take your time. Life is a singular journey.”

Peja (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube content creator, actress and model who rose to fame as a member of the ROCK SQUAD channel. She also has her own YouTube channel that has amassed over 1.3 million subscribers, where she mostly posts real life vlogs, GRWM and challenges with her family. One of her YouTube Shorts amassed over 90 million views. She has also been seen on the Nidal Wonder, Jordan Matter, The Royalty Family and Rebecca Zamolo YouTube channels. 

Before Fame

She began posting on Instagram in November 2020. She made her YouTube debut on the Rock Squad channel. She was an All Star cheerleader for 9 years before she started posting on social media. She began modeling at the age of four, and has since walked in Paris, New York and Los Angeles Fashion Weeks.

Trivia

In the September of 2023, Peja released her first cover to Pink by Lizzo and then followed it with her first original song "Outside." She appears on her pejaanne Instagram account where she has over 200,000 followers. 

Family Life

She was born and raised in the foothills of Northern California and has two older brothers, Kade and Zane, and an older sister Tessa. Her mother can often be heard in her videos as she is Peja's videographer. 

Associated With

She appeared as a dancer in the music video for Ferran The Fashion King's song "One Shot." She also starred in a music video for Trey Makai's song "Falling Full Speed. She has also worked with Elliana Walmsley and Jentzen Ramirez.

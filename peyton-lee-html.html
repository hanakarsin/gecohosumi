Peyton Elizabeth Lee - Age, Family, Bio

Actress with credits in Scandal and Shameless who landed the lead role in the Disney Channel series Andi Mack. 

Before Fame

She trained as an actress at a couple of studios in Los Angeles, California in 2014.

Trivia

She was featured in commercials for Sprint, Carnival Cruises, JC Penny, and Petco. She has performed as a dancer in Hot Chocolate Nutcracker and The Oscars Concert.

Family Life

Her father is actor Andrew T. Lee. She also has siblings named Ella and Julian. She has been romantically linked to Milo Manheim.

Associated With

She was a guest-star on the Kerry Washington starring series Scandal.

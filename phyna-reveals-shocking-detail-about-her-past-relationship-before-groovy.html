Phyna Reveals Shocking Detail About Her Past Relationship Before Groovy

Winner of the Big Brother Naija ‘Level Up’ edition, Phyna, has opened up about her past relationship and how unlucky she has been with men.

Naija News reports that the reality TV during her media rounds spoke about her past relationships and how she had never been taken care of.

Phyna said she has never been in a relationship where men gave her money as she is always the one taking care of the man.

She noted that, she feels a woman can also take care of herself as long as whatever they feel for their partner is very valid.

She, however, stressed that giving in a relationship should be reciprocated, adding that when she get tired of being the only one giving, she left.

Meanwhile, Phyna has debunked claims athat she snatched Groovy, from Amaka stating that the reality TV star asked her out and she agreed.

According to her, the N100 million grand prize is much and she might consider sharing it with Groovy.

It is uncertain if Phyna is serious about sharing the money with Groovy but many fans have advised her on the need to make a good investment with the prize money.

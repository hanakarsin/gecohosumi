NNPC, Three Others Generate N28.02tn In Three Years NEITI

Mar 31, 2024 The sum of N28.02tn has been generated by four revenue-generating agencies of the Federal Government between 2017 and 2019. The Nigeria Extractive Industries Transparency Initiative disclosed this on Thursday. According to NEITI, the agencies are; Nigerian National Petroleum Company Limited, Federal Inland Revenue Service, Department of Petroleum Resources, now Nigeria Upstream Petroleum Regulatory Commission, and the Ministry of Mines and Steel Development. NEITI disclosed that N22.68tn was remitted to the Federation Account out of revenue generated.

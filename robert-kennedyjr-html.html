Robert F Kennedy Jr. - Age, Family, Bio

Son of US Senator Robert F. Kennedy and nephew to former US President John F. Kennedy. He is known for his environmental law practices and for hosting Ring of Fire, a nationally syndicated left-wing radio show. He has written three bestsellers and authored many more books.  He announced in 2023 that he was running for president.

Before Fame

He earned his undergraduate degree from Harvard College and obtained a Master of Law degree from Pace University.

Trivia

In 1998, alongside Chris Bartle and John Hoving, he formed Tear of the Clouds LLC, a bottled water company that began donating all profits to Waterkeeper Alliance. He has written three children's books. 

Family Life

He had two children with his first wife Emily Ruth Black and four kids with his second wife Mary Kathleen Richardson. He then married actress Cheryl Hines on August 2, 2014.

Associated With

He endorsed the 2004 Presidential campaign of John Kerry.

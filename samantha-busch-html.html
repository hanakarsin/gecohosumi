Samantha Busch - Age, Family, Bio

Lifestyle blogger whose fitness and fashion posts have become incredibly popular through her marriage to NASCAR superstar Kyle Busch.

Before Fame

After earning a bachelor's degree in psychology from Purdue University, she went on to achieve her master's degree in industrial and organizational psychology in 2012.

Trivia

In addition to her blog, she also works with the Kyle Busch Foundation to supply children with gifts for holidays, cover the expenses of cancer patients, and a number of other charitable causes.

Family Life

She and Kyle Busch were married in 2010 and their son, Brexton Locke Busch, was born in May 2015.

Associated With

Her friend and fellow blogger Whitney Kay Scott is the wife of another race car driver, Brian Scott.

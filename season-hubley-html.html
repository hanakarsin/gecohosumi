Season Hubley - Age, Family, Bio

Best known for portraying Priscilla Presley in the 1979 television movie Elvis, this actress also appeared in the 1973 film The Lolly-Madonna War and in a 1972 episode of The Partridge Family.

Before Fame

Born Susan Season Hubley, she grew up in New York City with a sister named Sally and a brother named Whip (also an actor).

Trivia

She had a recurring role in the 1976 ABC series Family.

Family Life

Before divorcing in 1983, she and her first husband, actor Kurt Russell, became parents to a son named Boston Oliver Grant Russell. Her second marriage, to David Hayball, lasted from 1992 until 1994.

Associated With

She co-starred with George C. Scott in the 1979 movie Hardcore.

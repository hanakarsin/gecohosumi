See The Full Draws For The 2021/22 UEFA Champions League Group Stage

European football governing body, UEFA has announced the group stage draws for the 2021/2022 Champions League group stage games.

The draws for the group games were held on Friday at Instanbul, Turkey.

Naija News reports that the group games will feature 32 teams and the matches will begin September 14/15 this year.

Manchester City and PSG have been drawn together in group A in what observers have termed this season’s group of death.

Below is the full draws.

Group A

Man City, Paris Saint Germain, RB Leipzig, Club Brugge

Group B

Atletico Madrid, Liverpool, Porto, AC Milan

Group C

Sporting Lisbon, Borussia Dortmund, Ajax , Besiktas

Group D

Inter Milan, Real Madrid, Shakhtar Donetsk, FC Sheriff

Group E

Bayern Munich, Barcelona, Benfica, Dynamo Kyiv

Group F

Villarreal, Man United, Atalanta, Young Boys

Group G

Lille, Sevilla, Salzburg, Wolfsburg

Group H

Chelsea, Juventus, Zenit St. Petersburg, Malmo

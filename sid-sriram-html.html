Sid Sriram - Age, Family, Bio

Music producer who won the Filmfare Award for Best Playback Singer for his version of "Ennodu Nee Irunthaal" in 2015.

Before Fame

He studied at the Berklee College of Music and graduated with a degree in Music Production and Engineering.

Trivia

He has frequently worked with Bharata Natyam dancer, Pallavi Sriram. He has over 2 million followers on his sidsriram Instagram account.

Family Life

His mother is Latha Sriram.

Associated With

He and Mohit Chauhan are both from India and Filmfare Award winners.

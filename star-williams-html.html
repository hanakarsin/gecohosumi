Star Williams - Age, Family, Bio

Cast member of the Lifetime series Bring It! who dances alongside her sisters as members of the Dancing Dolls team. She launched her Instagram account, sweet__reflection, in 2014, and she has earned over 470,000 followers since.

Before Fame

Her older sister Sunjai Williams began dancing at the age of five, when Star was just three years old.

Trivia

She attended Clinton High School in Clinton, Mississippi. She has spent time speaking with various youth groups worldwide.

Family Life

She is the twin sister of Shamia "Sky" Williams. Her full name is Shania StarJai Williams.

Associated With

Her mother is Selena Johnson and her father is JJ Williams.

Tamera Mowry - Age, Family, Bio

Actress best known for playing Tamera Campbell on the ABC sitcom Sister, Sister alongside her identical twin sister Tia Mowry. In 2014, she became a co-host on the Fox talk show The Real. 

Before Fame

She was born at 4:30 P.M., two minutes before Tia. 

Trivia

She starred in the Disney original movie Twitches in 2005 and the sequel Twitches Too in 2007. 

Family Life

She married Fox News Correspondent Adam Housley on May 15, 2011. The couple welcomed their first child, a son named Aden, in November 2012, followed by a daughter named Ariah in July 2015. 

Associated With

She provided her voice for the character of Esther in three episodes of the animated series Family Guy, created by Seth MacFarlane.

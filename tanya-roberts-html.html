Tanya Roberts - Trivia, Family, Bio

Actress who played Julie Rogers on the final season of Charlie's Angels. She also played a role in the James Bond film A View to a Kill.

Before Fame

She dropped out of school at age 15 to marry her boyfriend and hitchhike across the country.

Trivia

She played the role of Midge Pinciotti on That '70s Show.

Family Life

She married Barry Roberts in 1974.

Associated With

She starred in the movie series Charlie's Angels with Jaclyn Smith.

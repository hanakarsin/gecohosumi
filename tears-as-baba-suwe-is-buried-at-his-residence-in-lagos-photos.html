Tears As Baba Suwe Is Buried At His Residence In Lagos (Photos)

The remains of the late veteran Nollywood comic Actor, Babatunde Omidina popularly known as Baba Suwe have been buried on Thursday, in Lagos state.

The burial was done around 11:05 am at his residence in the Ikorodu area of the state.

The event was attended by family members including the late actor’s son, Adesola Omidina, and many prominent figures from the Nollywood industry.

Tears flowed freely from the faces of those who attended the burial as the late actor’s body was committed to the earth.

See some photos from the burial.

It will be recalled that the actor died on Monday, 22nd November 2021 as announced by his son, Adesola Morenikeji Omidina, who posted it via his official Instagram page, @omo_omidina.

Though the cause of his sudden death wasn’t disclosed, Baba Suwe was believed to have been battling illness for a long time before his demise.

Naija News reports Baba Suwe was born August 22, 1958 (63 years), in Lagos Island, Lagos, Nigeria.

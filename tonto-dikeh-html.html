Tonto Dikeh News Today | Tonto Dikeh Biography, Husband and Net Worth Updates on Legit.ng

  • Name: Tonto Charity Dikeh
  • Birthdate: June 9, 1985
  • Birthplace: Rivers, Nigeria
  • Occupation: Nigerian actress, singer, philanthropist and politician

Career

Tonto Dikeh’s rise to fame began when she won the TV talent show “The Next Movie Star” in 2005. After her win, she gained popularity in Nigeria and has since appeared in over 150 movies.

In addition to her acting career, she has also released seven songs among them, Ekebe, Sugar Rush and Jeje.

Children

Tonto Dikeh has one child, a son named King Andre Dikeh.

Husband

Tonto Dikeh was married to Olakunle Oladunni Churchill in 2015. The couple met at a nightclub in Lagos and had an instant connection.

Tonto said in interviews that Olakunle was the one who mended her soul. However, the couple divorced in 2017.

Family

Tonto Dikeh was born into a family of five children and is the third child. Her parents are Mr Sunny Dike and the late Mrs Veronica Dike.

Tragically, Tonto lost her mother when she was only three years old. Her father later remarried and had two more children.

Net Worth

Tonto Dikeh’s estimated net worth is around $500,000 (US).

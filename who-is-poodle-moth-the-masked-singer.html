Who Is Poodle Moth on The Masked Singer? She's Been Unmasked! (SPOILERS)

Poodle Moth on 'The Masked Singer' Revealed! It's a Star From 'This Is Us'! (SPOILERS)

We never thought we'd see a Poodle Moth on this show, and now we need to know who's inside of the impressive costume.

By Jamie Lerner

May 8 2024, Updated 9:02 p.m. ET

Spoiler alert! Poodle Moth has been revealed! Scroll down to find out who was behind the mask!

The fun of The Masked Singer comes in guessing who is bringing these inexplicable characters to life. The show first premiered in 2019 with celebrities such as Gladys Knight, LaToya Jackson, Donny Osmond, and more dressed in kooky costumes designed by Marina Toybina. Marina is really responsible for bringing these characters to life — from aliens and monsters to a Jack in the Box and now a poodle moth, there's never a shortage of visual creativity.

As we get deeper into the season, we're sure to meet more characters and more celebrities behind them. In Episode 3, a Billy Joel-themed night brought us the mysterious Poodle Moth. But who is the celebrity behind the mask?

Poodle Moth on 'The Masked Singer' — The Clues

Episode 3 Clues:

  • Cowboy hat
  • American Flag
  • Book
  • Says she was discovered
  • Six candles
  • Billy Joel Night clue: Opry. Poodle Moth said, "I felt like a big shot when I performed at the Grand Ole Opry."

Episode 4 Clues:

  • “Sometimes the ones you love th most leave without so much as a letter. My dad left a hole in my heart that I never have filled. I’d send him letters that were always returned to sender.”
  • Cat postcard with cat stamp
  • Comedy/tragedy masks
  • Jar of fireflies
  • TV Theme Night Clue: Gilmore Girls. Poodle Moth said, “I have a very special connection to The Gilmore Girls that hits very close to home.”

Episode 5 Clues:

  • Family sold her childhood home
  • The number three
  • Book called “An Extraordinary Moth”
  • Palm leaves
  • Shower Anthem Night Clue: Robin. Poodle Moth said, “Robin, working with your dad is part of one of the biggest moments of my career and my life.”

Episode 10 Clues:

  • Talked about getting her big break at a time when she had no money
  • Poodle Moth’s Quarter Finals Night Clue was a message from her sister: “Poode Moth, is there anything you can’t do? Seriously, from performing in that costume to earning a Golden Globe nomination, your talents are endless. Keep singing your heart out. I love you to the moon and back.”

Poodle Moth on 'The Masked Singer' — The Guesses

Spoiler alert! While we don't know for sure who is behind the Poodle Moth mask, the correct identity may be spoiled in the guesses below.

Episode 3 Guesses:

  • Robin: Trisha Yearwood
  • Rita: Wynonna Judd
  • Ken: Rita Wilson

Episode 4 Guesses:

  • Ken: Melissa McCarthy
  • Jenny: Lauren Graham
  • Robin: Connie Britton

Episode 5 Guesses:

  • Rita: Sharon Stone
  • Ken: Brooke Shields
  • Robin: Shania Twain

Poodle Moth was very nearly eliminated in Episode 5, but Rita saved her with the Ding Dong Keep It On Bell!

Episode 10 Guesses:

  • Rita: Sheryl Crow
  • Ken: Riley Keough
  • Jenny: Chrissy Metz

So, who is Poodle Moth on 'The Masked Singer'? The answer is...

Although Poodle Moth was saved from elimination once, she didn't get lucky twice and was eliminated in Episode 10. Who was behind the mask? It was This Is Us star Chrissy Metz! Jenny managed to suss out Poodle Moth's true identity after her final performance — we're so impressed!

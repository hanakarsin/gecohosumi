Youssef Msakni - Age, Family, Bio

Professional footballer known for his work with the Al-Duhail FC in the Qatar Stars League. He is also known for his work as a forward and winger for the Tunisia national team. 

Before Fame

His youth career began with the Stade Tunisien academy in 2004. He started playing his professional career in 2008 when he joined the Esperance Tunis football club. He then joined the Tunisia national team in 2010. 

Trivia

He is known for having won the Qatar Stars League award in 2018 for Best Player of the Year for his work with the Al-Duhil Football Club. 

Family Life

He was born and raised in Tunis, Tunisia. In July of 2017, he married Tunisian actress and model Amira Jaziri. 

Associated With

Other professional players known for their work with the Tunisian national team include Bilel Mohsni and Saphir Taider.

Yung Beef - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper who founded the Barcelona-based trap collective, PXXR GVNG. They're known for their mixtape titled A.D.R.O.M.I.C.F.M.S. 2, featuring hits such as "Motriles" and "Beef Boy."

Before Fame

He originally was part of the group Kefta Boyz and created PXXR GVNG at the age of 24.

Trivia

At the age of 17, he spent time in Marseilles and London where he first started making waves in the underground rap scene.

Family Life

He was born Fernando Gálvez Gómez in Granada, Spain.

Associated With

He and Yomel El Meloso have collaborated on music together.
